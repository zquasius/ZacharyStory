Once upon a time there was a man named Phil Swift. He was an ordinary man who lived an ordinary life. However, this all changed when he found the legendary Flex Seal line of products. As Phil was able to grow his Flex Seal products into a monopoly, he lived a lavish life. With his millions and millions of dollars, he purchased houses all over the country. Him and his friends would party all of the time as they celebrated Phil's success.
